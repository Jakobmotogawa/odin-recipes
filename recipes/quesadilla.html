What is a Quesadilla?

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish that consists of a tortilla filled with cheese (and often other ingredients, such as meat and veggies). Itâ€™s cooked on a griddle or stove. Full quesadillas feature cheese sandwiched between two flat tortillas. Half quesadillas, like the one in this recipe, is a single tortilla that has been topped with fillings and folded into a half moon shape.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables

How to make at home

  1. Season the chicken, then broil it until it is cooked through.
  2. Cook the vegetables with the broiled chicken.
  3. Layer the mixture onto half of the tortilla, then top with cheese.
  4. Fold the tortillas and bake until the cheese is melted.
  5. Cut the quesadillas into wedges.
